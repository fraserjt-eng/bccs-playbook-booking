Before You Book

A few things to know.

Quick Check-in (15 min) - You have a specific question or need a quick status update.
Standard Check-in (30 min) - You want to review progress, discuss next steps, or talk through a decision. This is the best default choice.
Working Session (1 hour) - You need to actually build something out together, like drafting a plan or working through a process.
Deep Dive (1.5 hours) - You're tackling something complex that needs extended focus, or you want facilitation with your team.
